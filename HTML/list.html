Uday's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • warm milk
  • yeast
  • granulated sugar
  • egg and egg yolk
  • butter
  • bread flour

For the filling:

  • dark brown sugar
  • ground cinnamon
  • butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix milk, yeast, sugar and egg.
  2. Melt butter and add to mixture.
  3. Add flour in mixture.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer dough into large bowl.
